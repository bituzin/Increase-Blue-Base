What is Increase Blue

i
Increase Blue is a simple DApp running on the Base blockchain that allows users to increment a counter through on-chain transactions. Each increase is permanently recorded on the blockchain.

How to Use

i
  1. Click "Connect Wallet" to connect your Base wallet
  2. Once connected, use the "Increase" button to increment the counter
  3. Your transaction will be confirmed on the Base blockchain
  4. You can disconnect your wallet at any time
